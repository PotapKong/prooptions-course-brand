# Logo reference: Pro Options for Beginners

Canonical reference file:

`brand/logos/pro-options/pro-options-for-beginners-logo-reference.svg`

Spec file:

`brand/logos/pro-options/logo-spec.json`

## Visual rules

The logo must stay close to the uploaded reference:

- square dark canvas;
- centered logo group;
- text `ПРО ОПЦИОНЫ`;
- `ПРО` in white;
- `ОПЦИОНЫ` in warm gold;
- short gold divider line under the main title;
- subtitle `для начинающих` in white;
- thin geometric sans;
- wide letter spacing;
- no icons, no market symbols inside the logo group.

## Colors

- background: `#070A0D`;
- soft center glow: `#101923`;
- white text: `#F1F1F3`;
- gold text and line: `#EDB65B`.

## Usage in course visuals

For final images, use the logo file as a placed reference/asset, not as a prompt-only description.

If a generative model must recreate it, include this exact instruction:

```text
Use the provided logo reference exactly: centered thin geometric sans, wide tracking, ПРО in white, ОПЦИОНЫ in warm gold, short gold divider, subtitle для начинающих in white. Do not add icons or change text.
```

## Do not

- do not make the logo bold;
- do not compress the letter spacing;
- do not recolor `ПРО` or `ОПЦИОНЫ`;
- do not add candles, arrows, money or charts into the logo;
- do not place the logo over noisy terminal screenshots without dark overlay;
- do not replace it with generic text.
